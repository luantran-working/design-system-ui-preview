Built on Carbon Design System

Carbon is IBM's open-source design system for products and digital experiences. With the IBM Design Language as its foundation, the system consists of working code, design tools and resources, human interface guidelines, and a vibrant community of contributors.

Every component follows strict accessibility standards, ensuring your applications work for everyone. The 2x grid system provides mathematical precision and visual consistency across all screen sizes.
