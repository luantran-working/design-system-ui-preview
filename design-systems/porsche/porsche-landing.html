75 Years of Excellence

In 1948, Ferry Porsche created the first car to bear his family name. The 356 was a lightweight, nimble sports car that established the Porsche philosophy: performance through efficiency, innovation through engineering.

From Le Mans victories to everyday driving pleasure, Porsche has remained true to its founding principles. Every model carries forward this legacy of excellence, combining timeless design with cutting-edge technology.

Today, Porsche continues to push boundaries, leading the transition to electric mobility while preserving the soul of the sports car. The future is electric, but the passion remains pure Porsche.
