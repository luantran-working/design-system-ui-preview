Safety by design

Built on Constitutional AI principles

At Anthropic, we believe AI should be helpful, harmless, and honest. Claude is trained using Constitutional AI, a novel approach that builds safety directly into the model's behavior rather than relying solely on external filters.

Thoughtful Responses

Claude considers the implications of its outputs and aims to be genuinely helpful while avoiding potential harms.

Transparent Limitations

When Claude doesn't know something or isn't certain, it says so clearly rather than making up information.

Privacy Focused

Your conversations are private. We don't train on your data without permission and maintain strict data handling practices.

Ongoing Research

We continuously research and improve AI safety, publishing our findings and contributing to the broader AI safety community.
