Design Principles

The philosophy behind Soft design system

Comfort First

Every design decision prioritizes user comfort. Soft colors, generous spacing, and gentle transitions create an environment where users feel at ease.

Visual Harmony

Colors and components work together in perfect harmony. No element fights for attention; everything flows naturally.

Approachable Design

Rounded corners and soft shadows make interfaces feel friendly and inviting, encouraging user interaction and exploration.

Gentle Feedback

Interactions provide clear but subtle feedback. Hover states, focus indicators, and transitions guide without overwhelming.

Breathing Room

Generous whitespace and comfortable padding reduce cognitive load and improve content comprehension.

Consistent Experience

A cohesive design language across all components ensures users always know what to expect.
