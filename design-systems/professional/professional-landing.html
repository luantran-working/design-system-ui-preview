Design Principles

The foundation of our design system

Clarity Over Cleverness

Every component prioritizes clear communication and predictable behavior. Users should never wonder what an element does or how to interact with it.

Consistency Builds Trust

Consistent patterns, spacing, and interactions create familiarity. When users know what to expect, they can focus on their goals instead of learning your interface.

Accessibility is Non-Negotiable

Every user deserves equal access. Our components meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards with keyboard navigation, screen reader support, and proper focus management.

Performance Matters

Fast interfaces feel professional. We optimize every component for minimal bundle size, efficient rendering, and smooth interactions across all devices.

Flexibility Within Constraints

Design tokens provide customization without chaos. Adapt the system to your brand while maintaining the structural integrity that ensures quality.

Documentation is Design

A component without documentation is incomplete. Clear guidelines, code examples, and usage patterns ensure teams can implement correctly and confidently.
