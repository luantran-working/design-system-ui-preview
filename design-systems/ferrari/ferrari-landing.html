The Ferrari Legacy

Enzo Ferrari founded Scuderia Ferrari in 1929 to allow amateur drivers to compete. In 1947, he built the first Ferrari road car, the 125 S. Today, Ferrari represents the pinnacle of Italian automotive excellence, combining racing heritage with uncompromising luxury.

Every Ferrari tells a story of passion, innovation, and the relentless pursuit of perfection. From the legendary 250 GTO to today's hybrid hypercars, the Prancing Horse embodies the spirit of Italian craftsmanship and engineering excellence.
