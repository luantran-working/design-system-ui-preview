Our Collection

Legendary Motor Cars

Each Rolls-Royce is a testament to over a century of uncompromising excellence, where every detail is meticulously crafted to perfection.

Phantom

The Pinnacle

The Phantom represents the zenith of luxury motoring. With its commanding presence and serene interior, it offers an unparalleled sanctuary of tranquility. Hand-crafted to your exact specifications, each Phantom is a unique expression of personal taste and refinement.

Ghost

Effortless Elegance

The Ghost embodies a more dynamic expression of luxury. Its minimalist design philosophy and advanced engineering create an effortlessly refined driving experience. A perfect balance of power and poise, the Ghost is designed for those who drive themselves.

Cullinan

Effortless, Everywhere

Named after the largest diamond ever discovered, Cullinan is the first all-terrain SUV from Rolls-Royce. It combines the marque's legendary luxury with unprecedented versatility, allowing you to explore the world without compromise.

Spectre

Electric Luxury

Spectre marks the beginning of a bold new chapter. Our first fully electric motor car combines the silent running our founders envisioned with cutting-edge technology. The future of luxury is electric, and it begins with Spectre.

Bespoke

Your Vision, Our Craft

The Bespoke Collective at Rolls-Royce exists to transform your most ambitious visions into reality. Our master craftspeople work directly with you to create a motor car that is uniquely yours, limited only by imagination.

From hand-painted coachlines to personalized starlight headliners depicting your night sky, every element can be tailored to reflect your individual story and style.

  • Unlimited exterior color palette
  • Bespoke interior materials and finishes
  • Personalized embroidery and monograms
  • Custom wood veneers and inlays
  • Unique Gallery art installations
  • Tailored starlight headliner configurations
Heritage

Over a Century of Excellence

Since 1904, Rolls-Royce has been synonymous with the very best. From the legendary Silver Ghost to today's Phantom, our motor cars have set the standard for luxury, craftsmanship, and engineering excellence. Each generation builds upon this proud legacy, ensuring that the Spirit of Ecstasy continues to grace the world's finest automobiles.
