A browser designed for how you work

Every detail crafted to help you focus on what matters

🎨

Beautiful by Default

Frosted glass surfaces, gradient warmth, and translucent design that adapts to your chosen theme color. Arc dissolves the boundary between browser and content.

📱

Sidebar-First Layout

Tabs, spaces, and bookmarks live on the left, not the top. Navigate your entire web with a glance, without losing vertical space.

⚡

Command Bar

Press ⌘T to summon the command bar. Search tabs, history, bookmarks, or the web instantly. Your browser responds at the speed of thought.

🎯

Split View

View multiple tabs side-by-side without juggling windows. Perfect for research, comparison, or keeping reference material visible.

🔒

Privacy First

Built-in ad blocking, tracker prevention, and automatic HTTPS upgrades. Browse faster and safer without extensions.

🌐

Chromium Powered

All your favorite Chrome extensions work in Arc. Built on Chromium for compatibility, reimagined for productivity.
