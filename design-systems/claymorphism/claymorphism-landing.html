Why Claymorphism?

🎯

Tactile & Playful

Soft, rounded shapes with inner and outer shadows create a malleable, clay-like appearance that invites interaction and engagement.

🎨

Modern & Colorful

Vibrant color palettes and gradient surfaces bring life to your interfaces while maintaining excellent readability and accessibility.

⚡

Highly Usable

Clear hierarchy, consistent spacing, and predictable interactions ensure your users can navigate with confidence and ease.

📱

Fully Responsive

Designed mobile-first with fluid layouts that adapt beautifully across all screen sizes and devices.

🔧

Design Tokens

Built on a foundation of systematic design tokens for colors, typography, spacing, and effects - easy to customize and extend.

💫

Smooth Interactions

Subtle transitions and purposeful animations enhance the user experience without overwhelming or distracting.
