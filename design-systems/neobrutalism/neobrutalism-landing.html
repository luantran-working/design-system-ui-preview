DESIGN PRINCIPLES

The rules that make Neobrutalism work.

NO GRADIENTS

Flat colors only. Depth comes from shadows and borders, not smooth transitions. Every surface is solid and confident.

THICK BORDERS

4-8px borders define every element. Boundaries are clear, edges are sharp, and nothing bleeds together.

HARD SHADOWS

Offset shadows with no blur. 6-8px displacement creates depth without softness. Brutalist to the core.

HIGH CONTRAST

Text is readable, actions are obvious, and hierarchy is unmistakable. Accessibility through boldness.

SYSTEMATIC SPACING

4/8/12/16/24/32px scale keeps rhythm consistent. No arbitrary gaps, no visual chaos.

BOLD TYPOGRAPHY

Heavy weights for headlines, clear weights for body. Inter at 700-900 for impact, 400-500 for readability.
