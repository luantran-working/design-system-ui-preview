Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Revolut

How do I open an account?

Download the Revolut app, verify your identity with a photo ID, and you're ready to go. The entire process takes less than 5 minutes.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. We're transparent about all costs. Standard accounts are free, and Premium/Metal plans have clear monthly fees with no surprises.

Is my money safe?

Yes. Revolut is licensed and regulated by financial authorities. Your deposits are protected, and we use bank-grade security to keep your money safe.

Can I use Revolut abroad?

Absolutely. Revolut works in 200+ countries. Exchange currencies at interbank rates, withdraw cash from ATMs, and pay with your card anywhere.

How does currency exchange work?

Exchange 40+ currencies at the real interbank rate with no markup. Premium and Metal customers get even better rates and unlimited exchanges.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Premium and Metal subscriptions can be canceled anytime with no penalties. Your Standard account is always free.
