Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Cursor

What makes Cursor different from other AI coding tools?

Cursor is built from the ground up as an AI-first editor. Unlike plugins or extensions, AI is deeply integrated into every part of the editing experience. We understand your entire codebase context, not just the current file.

Is my code private and secure?

Yes. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and never train on your code. You can choose to use cloud models or run everything locally. Business plans include additional security features like SSO and audit logs.

Can I use Cursor with my existing projects?

Absolutely. Cursor works with any programming language and framework. It's built on VS Code, so you can import your existing settings, extensions, and keybindings seamlessly.

What AI models does Cursor use?

We support GPT-4, GPT-3.5, Claude 2, and other leading models. Pro users can choose their preferred model. We're constantly adding support for new models as they become available.

Do I need an internet connection?

For cloud models, yes. However, Pro users can configure Cursor to use local models that work completely offline. The editor itself works offline for all standard editing features.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period, then automatically revert to the free Hobby plan.
