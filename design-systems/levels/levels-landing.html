The Science of Metabolic Health

Understanding glucose is key to optimizing your health

Why Glucose Matters

Glucose is your body's primary energy source. When glucose levels spike and crash, you experience energy dips, brain fog, and increased inflammation. Stable glucose levels lead to sustained energy, better focus, and improved long-term health outcomes.

  • Reduces risk of chronic disease
  • Improves energy and mental clarity
  • Supports healthy weight management
  • Enhances athletic performance
  • Promotes better sleep quality

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Levels

Do I need a prescription?

No prescription needed. Levels provides access to CGM sensors through our medical team. We'll guide you through the simple onboarding process.

Is the sensor painful?

Not at all. The sensor is applied with a simple applicator and most members report feeling nothing. It's waterproof and stays on for 14 days.

How accurate is the data?

We use FDA-approved CGM sensors that are clinically accurate. The data is the same quality used by people with diabetes to manage their health.

Can I use Levels if I have diabetes?

Levels is designed for metabolically healthy individuals looking to optimize. If you have diabetes, please consult your doctor before using Levels.

What's included in my membership?

Your membership includes CGM sensors, the Levels app with personalized insights, meal scoring, trend analysis, and access to our support team.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans can be canceled anytime. Annual plans are non-refundable but you can pause your membership if needed.
