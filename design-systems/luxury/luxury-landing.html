Premium Design System

LUXURY

A high-end dark aesthetic with bold headings, monochromatic palette, and premium feel for luxury brand experiences. Modern, bold, and unmistakably exclusive.

Core Principles

Designed for Excellence

Every element crafted with precision, every detail considered. The Luxury design system embodies sophistication through minimalist elegance and bold typography.

Bold Typography

Oswald font family with expressive scale. Headlines carry personality while body text optimizes scanability and contrast for maximum impact.

Monochromatic Palette

Pure black and white foundation with subtle grays. Primary color #FAFAFA for emphasis, creating timeless elegance and visual clarity.

8pt Grid System

Consistent vertical rhythm across all sections. Predictable spacing and alignment create harmonious layouts without ad-hoc offsets.

Premium Components

Consistent radii, spacing, and elevation strategy. Strong focus states and predictable interactions ensure professional user experience.

Purposeful Motion

Subtle transitions with stable easing. 150-250ms duration emphasizes primary actions without distraction or unnecessary flourish.

Clear Hierarchy

Obvious content structure: headline, support text, primary action. Whitespace separates concerns before adding borders or shadows.
